Jen Hammelman

About

I am a senior at Tufts University studying computer science and biology. Currently applying to Ph.D. programs in systems biology, where my interest lie in solving complex biological phenomena by modeling and quantifying the dynamic changes to information within and between cells.

I am also interested in communicating science to a general audience, and you can find me working as a writer and the Chief Content Editor for Breakthrough Undergraduate Research Magazine and for the Odyssey Online. See my CV for more information
