Things I do

I am a student currently pursuing a bachelors of science in Computer Science and Biology at Tufts University, class of 2016. I am the Chief Content Editor of Breakthrough, the Tufts Undergraduate Research Magazine (I also helped with the current website design). I also have been a part of Tufts Symphony Orchestra and Tufts Third Day Gospel Choir.

Research Interests

I am currently interested in computational systems biology research, particularly to do with 3-d shape formation in developmental and regenerative biology. I am interested in how the big ideas in computer science and cognitive science can be applied to model biological phenomena. If interested, check out the work that is being done in the Levin lab at Tufts.
